About attorney

JUDr. Filip Geleta is an attorney residing in Banská Bystrica, Slovak Republic, practicing throughout the whole area of Slovak Republic. In 2016, he completed his master's degree with honors at the Faculty of Law of Comenius University in Bratislava by taking the state exam and, after completing his legal internship, he passed the bar exam with honors in 2020.
In 2021, he was awarded the title of JUDr.(iuris utriusque doctor) after defending his rigorous thesis. Since August 2021, he has been working as a bankruptcy administrator.
JUDr. Filip Geleta actively publishes and speaks at legal scientific conferences in the field of criminal law and administrative punishment.
